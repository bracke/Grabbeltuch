The Workspace is the central page of Grabbeltuch. All work takes place here and it contains almost all functionality.
The Workspace presents an overview of all your documents, appointments, notes, tasks and more.
The Workspace is divided into panels.

Panels

The workspace contains a series of panels, each containing a specific itemtype and optimised for displaying that particular itemtype.
